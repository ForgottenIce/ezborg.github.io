Currently on the site are CTGP custom track records and some of their statistics.

Also a Top 10 checker or Timesheet Downloader.

There are both no-glitch and glitch records. Some of the records may have unintentional shortcuts because the records are derived directly from the chadsoft API. Record Statistics can be found beneath the records on the records page and can be navigated to from a hyperlink on the top of the records page. When the database is running slow, pages will take longer to populate or not at all.

Many of the current or previous records have been recorded and are uploaded to youtube here: 150cc Records and 200cc Records.
Use ctl+f to search for a specific track.
